Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

iloveclaremontca.com ("we," "us," or "our") is a local guide to Claremont, California. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your rights.

What We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide:

  • Email address — when you subscribe to our newsletter
  • Phone number (optional) — only if you choose to provide it for text updates

We do not collect browsing data, use tracking cookies, or run analytics that identify individual users.

How We Use Your Information

Your email and optional phone number are used solely to:

  • Send occasional newsletters about new guides, updated content, and Claremont community highlights

We will never use your information for any other purpose.

Who Has Access to Your Data

Your email is stored securely by Buttondown, our email newsletter service. We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties.

Your Rights Under California Law (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

Unsubscribing

Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can also contact us directly to request removal from our list.

Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, email us at the address below. We will remove your information within 30 days.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, email us at iloveclaremontca@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
