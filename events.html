Events & Happenings

There's always something worth showing up for

February

Making Change Contest

An annual K-12 contest honoring Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. and champions of social justice. Students reflect on leaders who advanced equality — winners receive city recognition and awards.

April

Spring Egg Hunt

A Claremont tradition for families — egg hunts, games, and springtime fun. Hosted by the city at one of the local parks. Free and open to all ages.

May - June

Jacaranda Season

The iconic purple blooms peak in late May. Every street becomes a canopy of violet — Claremont at its most magical. Best photo spots: Indian Hill Blvd, College Ave, and the Scripps campus.

July

4th of July Celebration

Claremont's Independence Day celebration — live entertainment, food, family activities, and community spirit. A festive small-town Fourth you won't forget.

Summer

Concerts & Movies in the Park

Free Monday night concerts and outdoor movies all summer long. Bring a blanket, pack a picnic, and enjoy warm evenings under the trees. A Claremont summer staple.

August

National Night Out

Claremont's neighborhoods come together for an evening of block parties, food, games, and community building. A chance to meet your neighbors and celebrate the city's tight-knit spirit.

October

Halloween Spooktacular

Costumes, candy, games, and spooky fun for kids and families. The city's annual Halloween bash — a can't-miss for little ones.

October

Village Venture

Claremont's biggest annual street fair — 400+ craft vendors, food booths, live entertainment on multiple stages. The whole town comes out. Usually the last Sunday in October.

December

Holiday Promenade & Tree Lighting

Tree lighting ceremony, carolers, hot cocoa, and a stroll through the decorated Village streets. Usually the first Friday in December. Small-town holiday magic at its finest.
